Features

FarmBuddy is your smart farming companion. It provides farmers with personalized crop suggestions based on soil and weather conditions, helps detect plant diseases using image analysis, and delivers real-time weather updates. With planning tools and expert guidance, FarmBuddy empowers farmers to make informed decisions for healthier crops and higher yields.
